Moon Walk

A game featuring a miniature Solar System, the player can walk and jump around on a miniature Moon and observe the motions of orbiting rockets and the Earth and Sun. It was inspired by the Earthrise photo taken during the Apollo 8 mission. The game is built using OpenGL and C++.
