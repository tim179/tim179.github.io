Captcha Recognition

Developed a personal neural network class and tested it on recognition of captcha digits. An example of a captcha digit is shown below.

Digit 9

A 1000 captcha samples (a 100 per digit) have been normalized and split 80:20 (80% for training and 20% for testing). Below is the result of training a neural network with a sigmoid activation function and a squared error loss function. Each iteration denotes a weight update using a batch of 10 samples.
